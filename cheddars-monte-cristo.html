Ingredients:
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of smoked turkey
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 2 slices of Monterey Jack cheese
  • 2 cups prepared pancake mix
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • vegetable oil for frying
Instructions:

  1. Make the sandwiches ahead of time. Make two sandwiches with 2 slices of smoked turkey, 2 slices of ham, and one slice of American cheese, and one slice of Monterey jack cheese.
  2. Wrap the sandwiches individually snuggly with plastic wrap. Place the sandwich in the refrigerator for at least one hour, or as long as overnight.
  3. Preheat oil to about 320 degrees.
  4. Make enough pancake batter to prepare about six 4-inch pancakes. Gently unwrap the sandwiches and dip into the batter.
  5. Place the battered sandwich in hot oil and cook until golden. Flip the sandwich over so both sides brown evenly.
  6. Place the sandwich on a wire rack to drain. Allow the sandwich to rest for about 1 minute, then cut the sandwich, and dust with powdered sugar if desired.
